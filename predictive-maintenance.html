Powerful Predictive Capabilities

Failure Pattern Recognition

AI identifies recurring failure signatures in sensor data — detecting degradation patterns long before operators would notice them.

Real-Time Health Scores

Each asset is assigned a live health score (0–100) based on current sensor readings compared to healthy baselines.

Automated Work Orders

When a failure risk is detected, the system automatically creates and assigns a work order to the right technician.

Spare Parts Forecasting

Predict what parts will be needed before failures occur — so the right inventory is stocked and lead times are planned in advance.
