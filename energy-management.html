Key Features

Comprehensive Energy Intelligence

Live Energy Dashboards

Visualise electricity, gas, and water consumption in real time — drill down by plant, floor, department, or individual machine.

Peak Demand Management

Identify and reduce peak demand charges by automatically shifting non-critical loads to off-peak tariff periods.

Anomaly & Waste Detection

Automatically flag abnormal consumption — equipment left running, leaks, or inefficient processes — and alert the right people.

Target vs. Actual Tracking

Set energy budgets by department or asset and track real-time performance against targets — with variance alerts and trend reports.

Renewable Integration

Track self-generation from solar, wind, or captive power plants alongside grid consumption — with net energy and ROI reporting.

Automated Reporting

Auto-generate daily, monthly, and annual energy reports for management, utility regulators, and ESG frameworks without manual effort.
